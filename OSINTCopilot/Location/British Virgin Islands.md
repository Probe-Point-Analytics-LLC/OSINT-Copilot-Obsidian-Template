## Notes

The British Virgin Islands is a major offshore financial center and the jurisdiction where many Panama Papers shell companies were incorporated.

### Financial Significance
- One of the world's largest offshore financial centers
- Over 400,000 active companies registered
- Popular for shell company incorporation
- Minimal disclosure requirements
- No corporate income tax

### Panama Papers Connection
- Many Mossack Fonseca clients incorporated BVI companies
- PANAMA PAPERS LTD incorporated here October 31, 2007
- Preferred jurisdiction for asset concealment

### Risk Factors
- High-risk jurisdiction for AML/CFT
- Limited beneficial ownership transparency
- Frequently used in money laundering schemes
